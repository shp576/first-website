Recipe #1

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
lemon 1 full
salt 1/4 teaspoon
sugar 1/2 teaspoon
mint few leaves
masala 1/4 teaspoon

Step 1: squeeze lemon juice in two glasses.

Step 2: add the remaining ingredients one by one(crush mint before adding).

Step 3:add a few ice cubes and stir.

Recipe #2

  • Time:15 minutes
  • Serves: a party
Ingredients Quantity
sugar 1 full cup
gelatin 1/2 teaspoon
flavouring 1 teaspoon
colouring few drops

Step 1: melt sugar on stove in medium flame

Step 2:add the ingredients slowly when still hot and pour into mould

Step 3:let it cool for an hour and serve

Recipe #3

  • Time: 2 Hours
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
cookies 2 cookies
ice cream of favourite flavour 250 ml
toppings of choice if required go crazy

Step 1:cut ice cream into block and place it in between the cookies.

Step 2: put it in freezer to set for an hour.

Step 3:add you favourite toppings and enjoy.

Recipe #5

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2 pepole
Ingredients Quantity
wheat flour 1/2 cup flour
water 1/4 cup
mint bunch of leaves

Step 1:add water to flour little by little along with mashed mint.

Step 2: mix until a dough is formed and make small balls,roll the ball into a plain circle.

Step 3:fry on a pan with a little oil then take out and serve.
